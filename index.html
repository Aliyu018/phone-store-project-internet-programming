About Our Store

Mallaha phone store is a top-quality cell phone store that will be located in CIU,Nicosia. It is jointly owned by Aliyu and Abdullahi. Mallaha phone store was established to make second hand cell phones available to the residents of CIU, Nicosia as well as neighboring states at very affordable prices. In as much as the major products that we will be made available to our potential customers are second hand cell phones, we will not be stopping at just cell phones. We will also intend to make cell phone accessories such as chargers, earpieces, and cell phone batteries available to our potential customers. At Mallaha phone store, we intend to employ individuals with good communication skills. This we believe will help us retain our customers effortlessly.on.
